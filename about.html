How We Started

2023

The Spark & Conception

Founded in a small workspace, driven by the goal to simplify product catalog curation and make premium products affordable.

2024

Official Platform Launch

ShopSphere version 1.0 went live with 5 departments. We received positive reviews on our responsive checkout flow and dark mode design.

2025

Expanding Operations

Reached a milestone of 100,000 active shoppers. Opened regional delivery centers to guarantee next-day arrival for flash sale items.

2026

Targeting the Future

Pioneering browser-based local state persistence APIs and expanding catalog limits into international fashion and beauty.
